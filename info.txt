Controller: Manages HTTP requests and responses.
Entity: Represents data objects mapped to database tables.
Repository: Provides CRUD operations on entities.
Service: Contains business logic.
Exception: Handles errors and custom exceptions.
Utils:Contains utility classes and methods.
